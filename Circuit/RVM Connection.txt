RVM System Connection:

Servo-1: Signal -> GPIO 4 , VCC -> Buck 5V , GND -> Buck GND
Servo-2: Signal -> GPIO 5 , VCC -> Buck 5V , GND -> Buck GND

Green LED   VCC -> GPIO 12 , GND -> SMPS GND
Red LED     VCC -> GPIO 13 , GND -> SMPS GND
Red LED     VCC -> GPIO 18 , GND -> SMPS GND
Orange LED  VCC -> GPIO 21 , GND -> SMPS GND
Blue LED    VCC -> GPIO 22 , GND -> SMPS GND
White LED   VCC -> GPIO 23 , GND -> SMPS GND

Thermal Printer: RX -> RX , TX -> TX , VCC -> SMPS 12v , GND -> SMPS GND

ESP32: VCC -> Buck 5V , GND -> Buck GND

ESSP32 S3 CAM: USB Port to LAPTOP



Meaning of LED:-
Ready        -> Green LED
STOP         -> Red LED
PROCESSING   -> Yellow LED
SERVO OPEN   -> Orange LED
SERVO CLOSE  -> Blue LED
PRINT        -> White LED
